Morgan proyect.
Morgan is the captain in charge of managing the cargo that embarks on the ship "El Pirata".
The boat can only carry 28,000 kilos of cargo per trip. 
“El Pirata” provides its services only to 5 large companies in the region, who constitute its special clients and charge them per kilo and by type of cargo.


The application must allow:
registrate the clients.
Loading the boat, taking into account the conditions.
it is important to inform the company of the total value to be paid for the transport of the cargo.
Unload the boat to start a new trip.
Give the total weight of the cargo carried by the ship.
Show if it is possible for the ship to set sail or not.
update the category of customers.
